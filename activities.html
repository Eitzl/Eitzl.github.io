Outdoors

Hiking →

I spend a lot of time on trails around Santa Cruz and the broader Bay Area. There's something about moving through terrain on foot — the pace of it, the way a route unfolds — that I find hard to replicate anywhere else. From quick afternoon loops to longer ridge-line days, hiking is a constant.

Camping →

Car camping, dispersed sites, and backpacking trips are all in rotation. I like getting far enough out that the trip requires some planning — route, water, weather — and far enough in that you stop thinking about everything else. California has no shortage of places to do this well.

Biking →

Mountain biking is my other main outlet. The trails around Santa Cruz — Wilder Ranch, DEMO, the Flow Trail — are some of the best in California. I ride mostly trail and enduro terrain, and the sport has pulled me to some corners of the hills I never would have found on foot.
