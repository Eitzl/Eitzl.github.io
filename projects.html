SSW Prediction Project
Atmospheric Science  ·  In progress

An ongoing project exploring machine learning and computational methods for predicting Sudden Stratospheric Warming (SSW) events. SSW events are abrupt disruptions of the polar vortex that can cascade down to produce significant surface weather impacts — cold air outbreaks, shifts in storm tracks, and extended cold spells. The project works with atmospheric reanalysis data to identify precursor patterns and extend useful forecast lead time beyond what current dynamical models provide.
