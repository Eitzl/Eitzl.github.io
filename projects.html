SSW Prediction Project
Atmospheric Science  ·  In progress

A machine-learning and data analysis project focused on predicting Sudden Stratospheric Warming (SSW) events and atmospheric river activity. SSW events can drive significant surface weather impacts including cold air outbreaks. The project explores pattern recognition in reanalysis data to improve lead time on these forecasts.
